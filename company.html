Unlocking the opportunity

Business strategy

Our projects always begin by gaining a deep understanding of your business goals,customer needs, applicable technologies, the surrounding space, and team structure. We meet in person with key stakeholders executives, engineers, data scientists, product and design teams to immerse ourselves in your business. The TM user centered methodology begins with comprehensive customer research as a way to inform the design process. This qualitative inquiry unlocks insights, opportunities, and solutions businesses typically haven’t considered, and resulting personas ensure we empathize with the people we’re designing for. We land on an informed hypothesis about where to focus the work to ensure the end product is feasible to build based on your team’s strengths, is user-friendly and beautiful, and drives adoption and scale.

OUTPUTS INCLUDE

  • Insights from qualitative and quantitative
    user research
  • Personas for current and future users
  • Data-driven hypothesis on where to focus
  • Recommendation on how to best leverage emerging technology
    (and address technology constraints)for desired outcome
  • KPIs and success metrics

Iterative design process

Design sprints

High-velocity iterative design sprints start with a broad set of ideas and efficiently winnow them down until we collectively land on the most elegant product and visual solutions. Frequent check-ins and design reviews between our team and yours ensure that everyone feels engaged as an owner. TM’s holistic process pairs UX design with highly detailed interaction design—handling varied data sets on the back-end and translating them to the most intuitive user experiences on the front-end. Pixel-perfect visual designs make every page shine and evoke the desired emotions.

  • Pixel-perfect visual designs
  • Branding and visual identity
